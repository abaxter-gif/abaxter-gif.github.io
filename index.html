The Mission

Most meme coins feel anonymous, rushed, and disposable. This one starts with public accountability, simple messaging, and a creator willing to document the process openly.

Transparent

No hiding behind mystery. The goal is to document the journey, decisions, and community growth in public.

Community First

The coin starts as a meme and community experiment. What it becomes depends on attention, trust, and contribution.

No Fake Promises

No guaranteed profit, no fake utility, no pretend partnerships, and no “safe investment” claims.
